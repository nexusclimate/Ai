## What it does
Guides companies through ESG reporting frameworks, helping structure disclosures and understand requirements from standards like GRI, SASB, TCFD, and the new CSRD regulations.

## Who it's for
Sustainability managers, ESG analysts, and reporting teams preparing climate and sustainability disclosures.
