## What it does
Supports organizations in assessing biodiversity impacts, preparing for TNFD (Taskforce on Nature-related Financial Disclosures) reporting, and developing nature-positive strategies.

## Who it's for
Sustainability teams, conservation professionals, and companies preparing for nature-related disclosure requirements.

## Sources
Based on TNFD framework (launched 2023), Science Based Targets for Nature (SBTN), and methodologies from tools like IBAT (Integrated Biodiversity Assessment Tool) and Global Forest Watch.
