## What it does
Persefoni’s climate management platform includes Persefoni AI: anomaly detection for large emissions datasets, a Copilot for carbon accounting guidance, and (coming soon) natural language–based emissions factor mapping. It supports enterprise GHG accounting and reporting aligned with common standards and frameworks.

## Who it's for
Sustainability and finance teams, compliance officers, and enterprises that need scalable, AI-assisted carbon accounting and disclosure.
