## What it does
EO Browser from Sentinel Hub is an interactive web application for exploring Sentinel and Landsat satellite imagery. It lets you search by location and date, visualize scenes in the browser, and download imagery. Useful for quick exploration of raw satellite data without writing code, and for selecting scenes before using APIs or other tools for bulk processing.

## Who it's for
Researchers, analysts, and educators who want an easy way to browse and download Sentinel and Landsat data, or who need a quick visual check before committing to larger data pipelines or Google Earth Engine workflows.

## How to use it
Open [EO Browser](https://apps.sentinel-hub.com/eo-browser) and navigate to your area of interest. Choose mission (e.g. Sentinel-2, Landsat), date range, and band combination. Visualize and download selected scenes. For programmatic access or large-scale analysis, use the Sentinel Hub APIs or Google Earth Engine.
