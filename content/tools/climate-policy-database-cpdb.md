## What it does
The Climate Policy Database (CPDB) is a collaborative policy implementation dataset by NewClimate Institute, tracking climate mitigation policies globally. It provides a structured database of climate policy instruments and their status, with download or API availability for many datasets. Coverage spans many countries and is well suited to comparative policy analysis.

## Who it's for
Researchers, policymakers, analysts, and developers who need richly structured policy metadata for summaries, dashboards, or AI-assisted analysis and briefing tools.

## How to use it
Go to [Climate Policy Database](https://climatepolicydatabase.org) to explore the dataset, filter by country or instrument type, and download or access data where available. Use the structured metadata to feed into your own analysis modules or GPTs for policy briefings and comparisons.
