## What it does
Climate Change Laws of the World is a search tool that compiles climate laws and policy texts from 196+ countries. It is hosted by research institutions and provides a searchable database of legal text, frameworks, and climate measures. It is well suited to understanding legal and regulatory context for policy briefings and compliance work.

## Who it's for
Legal and compliance teams, policy analysts, sustainability professionals, and researchers who need authoritative legal context for climate regulation across countries.

## How to use it
Use [Climate Change Laws of the World](https://climate-laws.org) to search by country, theme, or keyword. Draw on the compiled legal and policy texts to support regulatory summaries, compliance briefings, or AI-assisted analysis—always cross-check critical points with official sources.
