## What it does
The International Energy Agency (IEA) launched this GPT in December 2024 so users can explore the full World Energy Outlook 2024 report in conversational language. It is powered by an AI model trained on the report and answers questions about energy trends, electricity demand, and outlook scenarios.

## Who it's for
Policymakers, analysts, researchers, and energy professionals who need quick, report-grounded answers without scanning the full PDF.
