## What it does
Open Energy Information (OpenEI) is an open, government-backed semantic wiki for energy and policy data. It includes regulatory and incentive information and related resources. Content is public domain and searchable, with API access for integration. It helps link energy policy with climate targets and is useful for integrated policy and energy briefings.

## Who it's for
Energy and climate analysts, policymakers, developers, and researchers who need open energy policy data and want to connect it with climate targets and regulatory context.

## How to use it
Go to [OpenEI](https://en.openei.org) to search and browse energy and policy data. Use the catalog and APIs to pull data into your own tools or GPTs for energy–climate briefings and analysis.
