## What it does
Watershed’s Product Footprints solution uses AI to map purchased products to activity-based emissions factors, automating Scope 3.1 measurement and giving line-item transparency. It supports sustainable procurement decisions and supplier engagement with a clear view of product-level carbon.

## Who it's for
Procurement, sustainability, and supply chain teams in companies measuring and reducing Scope 3 emissions and building sustainable procurement practices.
