## What it does
Collect Earth Online is a free, open-source system for interpreting and visualizing satellite imagery over time. It provides web-based satellite image visualization and supports change detection and environmental monitoring. Use it to observe land cover and environmental changes, interpret trends, and support agriculture monitoring, deforestation tracking, and habitat change analysis.

## Who it's for
Researchers, conservation and forestry teams, and analysts who need an easy-to-use, browser-based way to view and interpret satellite imagery over time without running heavy desktop GIS or processing pipelines.

## How to use it
Go to [Collect Earth Online](https://www.collect.earth) to load and visualize satellite imagery for your area of interest. Use the time slider and comparison tools to interpret changes over time. Combine with field data or other Earth observation products for validation and reporting.
